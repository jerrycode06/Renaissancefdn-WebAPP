About Us

History

Since 2009 Renaissance has been working in the field of academics. Renaissance is excelling in classroom learning, board results and competitive teaching. Our students have qualified different competitive exams. We have been engaging students from almost all schools.

Mission

Building strong academic base of students in all disciplines. Focussing on academic improvement from class 5th to 12th in all subjects and streams. Preparing students for board exams and competitive exams. Generating leadership qualities to form future leaders. Cultivating excellent creative and communicative skills and inculcating competitive skills among students.

Vision

Renaissance foundation is an organization of human resource development and human welfare. It focuses on the organic evolution of children in the world. Renaissance foundation has been working since a decade in transforming the lives of children at micro level. Renaissance means ‘rebirth’ and ‘revival of learning’. Renaissance Foundation enlightens children by reviving their spirits to attain knowledge and build character which is based on spiritual and rational approach.
