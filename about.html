About Us

Renaissance foundation is an organization of human resource development and human welfare. It focuses on the organic evolution of children in the world. Renaissance foundation has been working since a decade in transforming the lives of children at micro level. Renaissance means ‘rebirth’ and ‘revival of learning’. Renaissance Foundation enlightens children by reviving their spirits to attain knowledge and build character which is based on spiritual and rational approach. Since 2009, Renaissance has been working for children. In academics, in creativity and in forming goals, Renaissance guides children with the help of young as well as experienced scholars and experts in distinct field of life.

History

Founded in 2009.Since the age of 11 years to the age of 18, Renaissance takes care of children. In academics, in creativity and in forming goals, Renaissance guides children with the help of young as well as experienced scholars and experts in distinct field of life.

Mission

Building strong academic base of students in all disciplines. Focussing on the academic improvement of students from class 5th to 12th in all subjects and streams. Preparing students for board exams and competitive exams. Generating leadership qualities to form future leaders. Cultivating excellent creative and communicative skills Inculcating competitive skills among students.
